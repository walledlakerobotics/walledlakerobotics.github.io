Welcome to the home of Walled Lake Robotics.

Who are we?

Our teams, 308 and 7178, are FIRST robotics teams based out of Walled Lake, Michigan. Walled Lake Robotics fosters a learning environment in which students are prepared for jobs that they are interested in pursuing in the future.

Walled Lake Robotics competes in both the OCCRA season and the FIRST season early in the year. Anyone from Walled Lake Western, Central, or Northern is welcome to become a part of our teams! If you would like to learn more or meet us, visit our contact page.

What's Robotics?

Robotics is an after-school varsity sport in which groups of high school students, guided by experienced mentors, collaborate to build a robot that can compete in a season-specific game. Each season provides a new and unique challenge! To learn more about our seasons, visit our OCCRA and FIRST pages.
